Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Coaching Institute

What are the batch timings available?

We offer multiple batch timings to suit different schedules: Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM), and Evening (4:00 PM - 8:00 PM). Weekend batches are also available for working students.

What is the batch size and student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small batch sizes of 25–30 students per class. Our student-teacher ratio is 15:1, ensuring personalized attention and effective doubt resolution.

How do you track student progress?

We conduct weekly tests, monthly assessments, and mock exams. Detailed performance reports are shared with parents, and quarterly parent-teacher meetings are held.

What is your fee structure and payment options?

Fees vary based on course and class. We offer quarterly, half-yearly, and yearly payment options. EMI facilities and sibling discounts are also available.

Can I get a demo class before admission?

Yes! We provide free demo classes for all courses so students can experience our teaching methodology before enrolling.
